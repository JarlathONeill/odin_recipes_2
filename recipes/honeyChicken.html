Honey Chicken

Description

A slowcooker recipe to make 'set it and forget it' Honey & Garlic Chicken with Noodles
  • Chicken Thigh/Breast (400g)
  • Tablespoon Light Soy Sauce (x7)
  • Tablespoon Sriracha Sauce (x4)
  • Tablespoon Hoisin Sauce (x1)
  • Tablespoon Honey (x7)
  • Ginger (5g)
  • Clove of Garlic (x3)
  • Tablespoon Cornflour (x1) + Tablespoon Water (x1)
  • Chicken Stock (100ml)
  • Dry Noodles (200g)

Steps

  1. Put all ingredients into slowcooker and cook on high for 4 hours
  2. Add noodles when serving

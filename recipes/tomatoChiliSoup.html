Slowcooker Tomato & Chili Soup

Description

A slowcooker recipe to make 'set it and forget it' Slowcooker Tomato & Chili Soup

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes (x12)
  • Salt
  • Chili (x1)
  • Garlic Cloves (x5)
  • Basil
  • Onion (x1)
  • Carrot (x2)
  • Celery Stick (x1)
  • Teaspoon Oregano (x1)
  • Tinned Tomatoes (x2)
  • Tablespoon Tomatoe Puree(x2)
  • Litre Vegetable Stock (x1)

Steps

  1. Put all ingredients into slowcooker and cook on high for 3 hours, or low for 5 hours
  2. Blend with hand blender

Slowcooker Chicken Curry & Noodles<

Description

A slowcooker recipe to make 'set it and forget it' Chicken Curry & Noodles

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast (x3)
  • Tin of light coconut milk (x1)
  • Tablespoon of curry powder (x2)
  • Tablespoon of tumeric (x1)
  • Teaspoon of ginger, chili and coriander (x1)
  • Teaspoon of five spice (x1/2)
  • Tablespoon of tomato puree (x2)
  • Cloves of garlic (x5)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Medium onion (x1)
  • Dry noodles (200g)

Steps

  1. Put all ingredients into slowcooker and cook on high for 4 hours
  2. Add noodles when serving
